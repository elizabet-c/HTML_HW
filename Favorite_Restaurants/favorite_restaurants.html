Welcome to my Favorite Restaurants Page

Below is a list of some of my favorite places to eat in order of most favorite to least along with some additional information about the food offered price point.

  1. True Food Kitchen
    • Healthy American style dishes with a fresh twist
    • Favorite Menu Item: Turkey Burger
    • Price Point: $$
  2. Kodo Sushi
    • Hole-in-the-wall spot known for their seared tuna specials
    • Favorite Menu Item: Kodo Roll
    • Price Point: $$
  3. SumoMaya
    • Trendy Mexican-Asian Fusion with Tapas dining style
    • Favorite Menu Item: Tuna Tostada
    • Price Point: $$$
  4. In N Out
    • We all know this one. Amazing fast-food burger joint.
    • Favorite Menu Item: Protein Style Cheeseburger with Onions and Pickles
    • Price Point: $
  5. My Kitchen
    • Mostly chaotic, but usually end up with something edible in the end
    • Favorite Menu Item: Over-Baked Tofu Stir-Fry
    • Price Point: $-$$$
