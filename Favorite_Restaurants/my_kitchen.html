Welcome to My Kitchen


A kitchen full of chaos and learning

I have grown up cooking with my mom. She cooks at an extremely fast pace so I needed to make sure I was ready to go handing her whatever utensil or plate she needed. Since then, I have always cooked at a much slower pace. This often leads to a dinner with food at different temperatures. It has definitely been a learning experience, and the stove still intimidates me. But I have learned how to bake tofu pretty well if I do say so myself.
